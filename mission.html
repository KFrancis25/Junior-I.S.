Mission Statement

"Empowering Girls of Color Through Code: Our mission is to break barriers and foster inclusivity in the tech industry by providing comprehensive resources, mentorship, and opportunities for girls of color to explore and excel in the world of coding. We strive to cultivate a supportive community that celebrates diversity, cultivates confidence, and equips young women with the skills and mindset needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital landscape. Together, we're bridging the gap and inspiring the next generation of innovators, creators, and leaders."
